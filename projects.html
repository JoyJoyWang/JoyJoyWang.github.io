Computer Vision Platform for Infrastructure Inspection

Developed a computer vision platform for automated detection of structural cracks from imagery and LiDAR point clouds. The system employs multiple state-of-the-art models including SAM (Segment Anything), BLIP, CLIP, CLIPSeg, Grounding DINO, and UNet-VGG16 for comprehensive crack detection and analysis.

Impact: Achieved 31% accuracy improvement over baseline methods. Successfully commercialized with UrbanTech Consulting Engineering P.C. for real-world infrastructure assessment applications.

Technologies: PyTorch, SAM, CLIP, BLIP, Grounding DINO, UNet, VGG16, LiDAR processing
